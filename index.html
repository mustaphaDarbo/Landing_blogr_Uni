Introducing an extensible editor

Blogr features an exceedingly intuitive interface which lets you focus on one thing: creating content. The editor supports management of multiple blogs and allows easy manipulation of embeds such as images, videos,and markdown. Extensibility with plugs and themes provide easy ways to add functionality or change the looks of blogs.

Robust content management

Flexible content management enables users to easily move through posts. Incase the usability of your blog by adding customized categories, sections, format. With this functionality , you're in full control.

State of the Art Infrastructure

With realibility and speed of mind, worldwide data centers provide the
backbone for ultra-fast connectivity. This ensures your site load
instantly, no matter where you readers are, keeping your site
competitive.

Free, open, simple

Blogr is a free and open source application backed by large
community of helpful developers. Its support feature such as code
highlighting.RSS feeds, social media integration, third-party
commenting tools, and works seamlessly with Google Analytics. The
architecture is clean and is relatively easy to learn

Powerful tooling

makes customization and deployment a breeze, but capable of
producing even the most complicated sites.
